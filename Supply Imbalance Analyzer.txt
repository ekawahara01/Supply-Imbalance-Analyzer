1. Product Purpose & Core Question

Supply Imbalance & Supply Decay Analyzer is a decision-support tool designed to identify underserved or structurally failing markets by analyzing the quality, sustainability, and cost-efficiency of existing supply in search results.

The core question this tool answers is:

‚ÄúDo existing solutions exist ‚Äî and if so, are they structurally failing to serve the market?‚Äù

This tool is explicitly designed to detect functional supply shortages, not demand volume.

2. Key Differentiation (Why This Tool Exists)

Traditional SEO tools (e.g., Ahrefs, SEMrush) focus on:

Domain authority

Keyword difficulty

Competitive strength

This tool focuses instead on:

Supply health

Supply decay

Supply cost inefficiency

Maintenance fragility

It identifies markets where:

Supply exists but is outdated

Supply is overbuilt relative to value

Supply is fragile, homogeneous, or unsustainable
